## Design Takeaways

1. Keep the repository small enough to explain in an interview: docs, templates, scripts, examples, and distilled skills.
2. Treat troubleshooting as a loop: facts first, scope second, hypothesis third, validation fourth, then communication and closure.
3. Make scripts produce readable sections and degrade gracefully when optional commands are missing.
4. Make templates directly usable during customer-facing support work.
5. Make skills compact and executable so an AI agent, collaborator, or future self can follow the toolkit without rereading every document.
6. Record attribution explicitly even when only ideas and structure were learned.
